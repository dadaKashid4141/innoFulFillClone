FAQs

For any queries, refer FAQs section.

How do I start shipping with Innofulfill?

You have to sign up, recharge your wallet and start placing orders within minutes. Our Sales team will give you a call within an hour and explain all the details. In case of any urgent queries, you can send your requirements to sales@innofulfill.com and we will reach out to you at the earliest!

Is same day delivery possible?

Yes, Innofulfill provides the hyperlocal delivery service and same day shipping is possible depending on the delivery location.

What if my store is not integrated with any e-commerce platform? Can I still ship with Innofulfill?

Yes, Innofulfill offers custom rest APIs that are easy to integrate seamlessly with your store.

Do I need to know how to code to integrate with Innofulfill?

No, Innofulfill offers ready to install customized rest APIs that connect your store with our platform. All you need to do is navigate to our channel integrations page that is easy to understand. In case you still face issues, you can contact our sales executive or write a mail to sales@innofulfill.com.

Will I be charged anything apart from shipping costs?

Innofulfill levies zero charges/costs to its clients. You only pay for what you ship.

How many days will it take to get my COD remittances?

We have a weekly remittance cycle. You will get your weekly remittance on the first day of next week.

Does Innofulill provide packaging?

No, clients have to complete the package on their own before they’re handed over to our delivery partner.

I do not have a GST number yet , can I ship products?

You can provide your PAN card until you get your GST number and start shipping.

Does Innofulfill provide the shipping labels for deliveries?

No, clients have to complete the package on their own before they’re handed over to our delivery partner.

How do I know my package has been successfully delivered?

Innofulfill sends clients timely email and SMS alerts to keep them updated about their order status.

Will my customers get alerts about their order status?

Yes, we send SMS and email communication to customers at regular checkpoints to keep them informed of their order status.
